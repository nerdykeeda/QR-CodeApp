Privacy Policy

How LinQrius protects and handles your personal information

1. Information We Collect

LinQrius is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Usage Data: Basic analytics to improve our service
  • No Personal Storage: We do not store your personal information on our servers
  • Local Processing: All data processing happens in your browser

2. How We Use Information

The information you provide is used solely for:

  • Generating QR codes and vCard files
  • Providing real-time preview of your visiting card
  • Improving our service functionality

3. Data Security

We implement several security measures to protect your information:

  • All data processing occurs locally in your browser
  • No personal data is transmitted to our servers
  • We use HTTPS encryption for all communications
  • Regular security audits of our systems

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Font Awesome icons (CDN)
  • Analytics to improve user experience

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Essential functionality
  • Analytics to improve our service
  • User preferences

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect some functionality.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we might have
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. International Transfers

Since we process data locally in your browser, no international data transfers occur. However, our servers may be located in different countries.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page.

10. Contact Us
